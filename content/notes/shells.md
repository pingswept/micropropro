## What is a shell? ##

A shell is the program that generates the terminal prompt that you see when you log in to a Raspberry Pi. The shell receives your commands and executes them for you.

Kernel vs. shell

* sh
* csh
* tcsh
* bash
* dash
* ksh
* zsh
* fish
